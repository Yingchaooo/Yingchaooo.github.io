  <section class="warm-section warm-about">
    <p>I am a Ph.D. candidate in Systems Engineering at the City University of Hong Kong. My research focuses on intelligent infrastructure monitoring and non-destructive pavement damage detection.</p>
    <p>I work on computer vision and vision-language models for pavement damage detection, crack segmentation, and structural health monitoring. I have published 12 journal articles and 3 conference papers in venues including <em>Computer-Aided Civil and Infrastructure Engineering</em>, <em>Automation in Construction</em>, and <em>IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems</em>.</p>
  </section>
