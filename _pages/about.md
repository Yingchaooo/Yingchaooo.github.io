  <h1>Yingchao Zhang</h1>
  <p class="intro-lede">I am a Ph.D. candidate in Systems Engineering at the City University of Hong Kong, working on intelligent infrastructure monitoring and non-destructive pavement damage detection.</p>
  <p>My research focuses on computer vision and vision-language models for pavement damage detection, crack segmentation, and structural health monitoring. I have published 12 journal articles and 3 conference papers in venues including <em>Computer-Aided Civil and Infrastructure Engineering</em>, <em>Automation in Construction</em>, and <em>IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems</em>.</p>
